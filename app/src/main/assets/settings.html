Settings


Log out on hide
Logs you out of Pardus whenever the app is in the background.
Full-screen mode
In full-screen mode the phone's status bar is hidden. No effect on tablets.
Show zoom controls
Shows zoom control buttons while scrolling. Always shown if your phone does not support multi-touch.
Remember page properties
Remembers the zoom level and scroll position of each visited page.
Nav horizontal tiles
Sets the width of the Nav space chart in tiles (11 for Premium accounts only).
Nav vertical tiles
Sets the height of the Nav space chart in tiles (9 for Premium accounts only).
Partial page refreshes
Only refreshes sections of the Nav screen that have changed. Prerequisite for ship animation.
Ship animation
Shows your ship's movement when changing location. "Partial page refreshes" required.
Ship rotation
Makes ships face the direction they are heading.
Chat lines limit
Limits the amount of loaded chat lines to 10.
Clear cache
Empties the cache including page-specific zoom levels/scroll positions and clears any stored user/password.
